Let's get your phone number

Your AI receptionist is built and ready. Your whole setup is just: 1) claim your free phone number, 2) point your AI at it, 3) put that number on your signs and ads. This page walks you through 1 and 2 with pictures — each one shows the exact button to tap. Bookmark this page so you can come back to it anytime.

✓ 100% FREE — WE PAY FOR YOUR NUMBER

Takes about 2 minutes • You only do this once

A Get your number
1

Open Phone Settings & click "+ Add Number"

In the left menu click Settings ⚙ → Phone System, then click the blue + Add Number button (top-right).

2

Choose "Add Phone Number"

A small menu appears — click the top option, Add Phone Number.

3

Pick any number you like

Tap the circle next to whichever number you want, then click Proceed to Buy.

💚 Don't worry about the $1.15 or the word "Buy." You are NOT being charged — your number is completely free. We cover it. Just click Proceed to Buy to keep going.
4

Verify your identity (one time, ~2 min)

Every U.S. phone number requires a quick, secure ID check. Click Begin verifying. The easiest way: choose "Continue on another device," scan the code with your phone, and snap a photo of your ID. When you see "You're done!", you're set.

🛡️ Why do I have to show ID? Activating any new U.S. phone number requires a one-time identity check — it's a standard telecom rule tied to federal anti-spam and anti-fraud regulations (the FCC's STIR/SHAKEN framework) that every carrier must follow. It's quick, secure, and handled by a trusted verification partner. OneVoice never sees or stores your ID.
⚠️ Right after your purchase, a "Configure & Register Number" box pops up saying "Number purchase successful!" with a blue "Register for A2P" button. Do NOT click that button — click the ✕ in its top-right corner to close it. A2P/10DLC registration is only for sending marketing text messages and is not needed for your AI to answer calls — it's a multi-day business-registration process you can skip entirely. If you see a red "A2P 10DLC registration is incomplete" banner afterwards, ignore that too.
🔄 Don't see your new number yet? Refresh the page. After you close that box, reload your browser tab — your number will appear under Manage Numbers.
💳 You should never be asked for a credit card. If a screen says "Add a payment method to continue," don't enter your card — close it or click away. Your number is on us. If it blocks you from continuing, call us at (855) 770-0200 and we'll clear it in a minute.
✅ Your number is yours — and free to you. Now let's point your AI at it.
B Connect it to your listing's AI
5

Open your AI Agent's "Deploy" tab

In the left menu click AI Agents, click your agent to open it, then click the Deploy tab at the top.

6

Assign your number

Click the blue Assign phone numbers link. In the pop-up, click the Please select dropdown, choose your number, and click Save.

🎉 All done! Your AI receptionist now answers that number and works your listing 24/7.
